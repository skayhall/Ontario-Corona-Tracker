About the Virus

What is it?

Coronaviruses also known as Covid-19 are a large family of viruses that can cause illnesses ranging from the common cold to more serious respiratory infections like bronchitis, pneumonia or severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS).

Where did it start?

The virus originated in Wuhan, China.
